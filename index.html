Training Steerable CNNs

Comparing standard and group-equivariant steerable CNNs (C2–C8, D1–D8) on Rotated MNIST to show how building rotational symmetry into network architecture improves generalization to unseen rotations. The best equivariant model (D8) achieved 97.24% accuracy on Rotated MNIST, compared to 26.0% for a standard baseline CNN.
